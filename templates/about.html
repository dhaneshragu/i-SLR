Why i-SLR ?

Deafness is not a tragedy. It's a community bound by a common language and a sense of pride.

Marlee Matlin

🤟 With approximately 65 million deaf individuals in India and nearly 20% of the global population facing deafness, communication gaps persist between abled and differently abled individuals. Sign language serves as a unifying tool, but its extensive regional variations pose challenges. 🌍 i-SLR aims to bridge this gap by detecting signs and assisting non-deaf individuals in practicing them, fostering closer connections with the loved ones of deaf individuals. 🤝👨‍👩‍👧‍👦💞
